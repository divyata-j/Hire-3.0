Blockchain Test


Thank you for your interest in joining Hire 3.0! Before we can consider your application, you will need to take our blockchain test to demonstrate your skills and knowledge in the field.


Test Details


  • The test will consist of 50 multiple choice questions.
  • The test will cover a wide range of topics, including blockchain fundamentals, cryptocurrency, smart contracts, and more.
  • You will have 60 minutes to complete the test.
  • The cutoff score for passing the test is 75%.
  • If you pass the test, you will be invited to join Hire 3.0 and connect with leading companies in the blockchain industry.

Tips for Success


  • Review the topics listed above and make sure you have a strong understanding of each one.
  • Try to familiarize yourself with real-world examples and use cases for blockchain technology.
  • Practice with sample questions and take online practice tests to improve your test-taking skills.
  • Make sure you are well-rested and prepared the day of the test to give yourself the best chance of success.

We wish you the best of luck on the test!
