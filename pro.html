Welcome to Hire 3.0


We connect top blockchain developers with leading blockchain companies. Here's how it works

  1. Developers fill out a registration form with their personal and career information, as well as their education background.
  2. Developers take a test to demonstrate their skills and knowledge of blockchain technology.
  3. If developers pass the test with a qualifying score, they will receive a link via email to join Hire 3.0.
  4. Upon joining, developers will have access to their dashboard, where they can see which companies their profile has been sent to.
  5. Companies can also join Hire 3.0 by filling out a registration form with information about their company.
  6. We screen all companies before approving their registration and sending them a link to join via email.
  7. Once they join, companies will have access to their dashboard, where they can view profiles of developers that have been sent to them and choose to accept or reject them.
  8. If a developer is hired by a company and their annual salary is more than INR 15 lakhs, the developer will be required to pay 17% of their salary to Hire 3.0 for the next 3 years. The company will also pay a lump sum to Hire 3.0.
